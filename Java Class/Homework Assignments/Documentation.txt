Game Summary:
  Player is a new employee on a martian mining colony.  As the game begins the facility is rocked by an explosion and beins to fill with poisionous gas,
  alien mosters and rival colony enemies.  The player must reach an escape pod before it is too late.  Along the way the player must find usefull tools and weapons,
  battle enemies and solve puzzels to get to safety.  The player will encounter multiple ways to solve problems, including a hacking mini-game.  Gameplay will include
  features like health meter, combat system, death penalties and respawn safe zones.
  
Objects used:
  Keys, office equipment, weapons, medical packs, upgrades, dispencers, money/credits
